Design @ Columbia

Spring 2021 - Current

As a core team member of Design @ Columbia, I was able to aid the student-run organization's goal of fostering a strong design community at Columbia University. The organization hosts panels, holds workshops and many other similar collaborative events that are meant to target students of all disciplines who are interested in design.
My role involved strategizing our organization's re-branding. This involved developing a new visual language for the organization. Working with the Marketing Coordinator Wendy Sung and Design Lead Laura Block, we drafted different languages and styles, using various iteration strategies.
